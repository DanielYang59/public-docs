# Filter by Formula/Composition

#### 1. [Go to Materials Project - Battery Explorer](https://materialsproject.org/batteries)

#### 2. Search the chemical formula of interest

#### 3. Click on Search

![Step 3 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/public/screenshot\_67c0b7fa-e48c-4280-ba26-a9d3bb845338.png?crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.7582\&fp-y=0.3304\&fp-z=2.8471\&w=1200\&mark-w=0.2\&mark-pad=0\&mark64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL21hZGUtd2l0aC10YW5nby13YXRlcm1hcmsucG5n\&ar=3584%3A1952)

#### 5. Select the working ion of choice

![Step 5 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/public/screenshot\_5b54acf7-c0a8-4eb9-93a9-46769ffdafc6.png?crop=focalpoint\&fit=crop\&fp-x=0.1473\&fp-y=0.6557\&fp-z=2.0235\&w=1200\&mark-w=0.2\&mark-pad=0\&mark64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL21hZGUtd2l0aC10YW5nby13YXRlcm1hcmsucG5n\&ar=3584%3A1952)
